Yoko Kayano and Saku Sugawara received the **Best Presentation Award** (1 out of 14) at the [19th AAMT Seminar / 1st AAMT Translation & Interpretation Research Workshop](https://aamt.info/event/seminar/20260318) (March 18, 2026) for their presentation "What Does Automatic Evaluation of Translation Quality Actually Evaluate?"
